# Pairwise Review: msr-01 vs msr-04

## Pair

- Candidate A: `msr-01`
- Candidate B: `msr-04`

## Reviewer Role

Reviewer checks CET product fit, next-action clarity, app visual grammar, interaction state cause, physical Space continuity, implementation mapping, and phone layout containment.

## Winner

Candidate A, `msr-01`.

## Visual Evidence

- Candidate A evidence: `candidate-proofs/mobile-reset-candidate-proof.html#msr-01`
- Candidate B evidence: `candidate-proofs/mobile-reset-candidate-proof.html#msr-04`

## Product Truth

`msr-01` preserves both current-card operation and Space continuity. `msr-04` is strong on operation clarity but weak on product distinctiveness.

## Task Clarity

Both make action clear. `msr-04` is slightly clearer for immediate answering, while `msr-01` is clearer for the full Learning -> result -> Space loop.

## Space Or Interaction Fit

`msr-01` better handles the relationship between action, result, and physical address. `msr-04` risks a generic quiz-app silhouette.

## Visual System Fit

`msr-01` has better softbook identity because the object rail and address aperture are visually distinctive. `msr-04` is safer but less memorable.

## Implementation Mapping

`msr-04` maps simply to action controls, but `msr-01` maps the complete app surface and avoids later rework for Detail and Space.

## Rationale

Choose `msr-01`. Borrow `msr-04`'s strict primary-action discipline so the winning synthesis does not become decorative or over-layered.

## Borrowable Fragments

- From `msr-04`: bounded primary action, reduced secondary controls, quiet ledger idea.
- From `msr-01`: object rail, result slip, Space aperture.

## Rejected Fragments

- From `msr-04`: utility-console tone and weak physical-space presence.
