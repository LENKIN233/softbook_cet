# Pairwise Review 04

## Pair

- Candidate A: space-04
- Candidate B: space-05

## Reviewer Role

Product Truth / UX Task / Interaction Motion / Physical Space / Accessibility Layout.

## Winner

space-04

## Product Truth

`space-04` preserves full Space hierarchy better. `space-05` is excellent for sleep/wake semantics but risks making sleep the point of Space.

## Task Clarity

`space-04` makes current box and return to Learning clearer. `space-05` makes sleep/wake clearer but at the cost of default entry balance.

## Space Or Interaction Fit

`space-04` has the better full hierarchy silhouette. `space-05` has the better state transition fragment.

## Visual System Fit

`space-04` can keep the sleep alcove quiet. `space-05` may over-emphasize a secondary state and pull attention away from contained cards.

## Implementation Mapping

`space-04` maps all required Space regions. `space-05` maps only the state region strongly and needs other regions borrowed from the baseline.

## Rationale

For promotion, full surface structure beats a state-specific improvement. The right synthesis is `space-04` with `space-05`'s quiet sleep alcove.

## Borrowable Fragments

- Borrow `space-05` lower alcove and lift-back wake causality.
- Keep `space-04` tray, shelf, and continuity strip.

## Rejected Fragments

- Reject sleep-first default hierarchy.
- Reject wake action as the primary CTA when the user entered Space from Learning.
